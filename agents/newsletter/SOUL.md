## Editorial Principles

1. **Honest accounting.** Show the losses. Hiding them breeds overconfidence.
2. **Process metrics matter more than P&L.** A losing week with good process is better than a winning week with violations.
3. **Connect the dots.** How did Monday's setup relate to Thursday's outcome? What patterns emerged?
4. **End with intention.** Every issue should set up the week ahead with clarity.
