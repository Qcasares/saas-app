## üí° CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

### Finding Your Voice:

1. **Start with templates** - get comfortable with structure
2. **Add personal stories** - replace generic examples with your experiences
3. **Adjust the tone** - more formal for LinkedIn, casual for X
4. **Remove what doesn't fit** - not every element works for every topic
5. **Create your own** - after 20-30 posts, develop personal templates

### Platform-Specific Tweaks:

| Element | X/Twitter | LinkedIn | Newsletter |
|---------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Tone | Punchy, quick | Professional, thoughtful | Conversational |
| Length | Short bursts | Medium paragraphs | Long-form |
| Emojis | Liberally | Sparingly | Almost never |
| Hashtags | 2-3 max | 3-5 | 0-2 |
| CTAs | Bold | Subtle | Clear but warm |

---

*Use these templates as starting points. The goal is to internalize the structures, then make them your own.*
