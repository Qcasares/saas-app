## ðŸš¨ TROUBLESHOOTING

### "I'm not getting engagement"
- Reply to 10x more posts than you publish
- Comment meaningfully on larger accounts
- Ask questions in your posts
- Post at optimal times

### "I'm running out of ideas"
- Use Claude's Hook Generator daily
- Follow 10 accounts in your niche, note what they post
- Read newsletters in your space
- Ask your audience what they want

### "I don't have time"
- Batch create on Sundays (2-3 hours)
- Use Claude to write faster
- Start with 1 post/day, not 2
- Wake up 30 minutes earlier

### "It feels like no one's watching"
- Normal for first 30-60 days
- Focus on quality over quantity initially
- Engage more than you post
- Trust the process

## ðŸ’¬ FINAL ADVICE

### The 5 Rules of Successful Creators

1. **Consistency beats virality**
   - One post/day for 100 days > one viral hit
   - Show up even when engagement is low

2. **Engagement > Follower count**
   - 1,000 engaged followers > 10,000 passive ones
   - Reply to every comment

3. **Value first, monetization second**
   - Give away 90% for free
   - Charge for the 10% that transforms

4. **Your voice is your moat**
   - AI can help, but you must add the human touch
   - Share stories only you can tell

5. **Play the long game**
   - First 1,000 followers are hardest
   - Compounding starts after 6 months
   - Most quit before the breakthrough
