## ‚ö†Ô∏è MONETIZATION MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Monetizing too early**
   - Build trust first
   - Wait until you have 500+ engaged followers
   - Focus on value, not extraction

2. **Promoting everything**
   - Only recommend what you use
   - Say no to 90% of sponsorships
   - Protect your reputation

3. **Underpricing**
   - You can always lower prices
   - Test higher prices
   - Premium positioning attracts better clients

4. **One income stream**
   - Diversify from day one
   - Aim for 3+ revenue sources
   - Protect against platform changes

5. **Ignoring taxes**
   - Set aside 25-30% for taxes
   - Track income from day one
   - Consider forming an LLC
