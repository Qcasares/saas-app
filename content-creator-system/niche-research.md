## Key Research Insights

### What Makes Content Go Viral (2024-2025)
1. **First 3 seconds** determine 80% of engagement
2. **Contrarian takes** with data backing perform best
3. **Personal stories** with universal lessons
4. **Practical, actionable** beats inspirational
5. **Visual formats** (carousels, threads) boost sharing

### What Builds Sustainable Audiences
1. **Consistency** over virality
2. **Niche depth** over broad appeal
3. **Community engagement** (replying, asking questions)
4. **Newsletter ownership** (not just social following)
5. **Value-first monetization**

### Platform Priorities (2024-2025)
| Platform | Content Type | Posting Frequency | Monetization |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|--------------|
| X/Twitter | Threads, hot takes, conversations | 1-3x daily | Subscriptions, affiliate |
| LinkedIn | Long-form posts, carousels | 1x daily | Newsletter, B2B services |
| Newsletter | Deep dives, resources | 1-2x weekly | Ads, products, subs |
