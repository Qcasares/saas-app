### Headline
The AI Value Gap: 88% of enterprises have adopted AI. Only 5-10% see significant ROI.

### Body
The numbers tell a sobering story.

Enterprise AI adoption has hit 88% — a rate that surpasses cloud and mobile adoption curves. Yet the value remains frustratingly elusive.

72% of organizations are now formally measuring Gen AI ROI. They're trying. But here's what the data reveals:

→ 46% of AI POCs never reach production
→ Workers report 40-60 minutes saved daily (10% productivity gain)
→ Yet only 5-10% achieve the transformative returns they projected

The gap isn't in the technology. It's in the approach.

High performers — those investing $10M+ and scaling across business units — report 71% likelihood of significant gains vs. 52% for smaller bets. They're not experimenting more. They're committing deeper.

Three patterns separate the value-capturers from the adopters:

1. **Ruthless prioritization** — They kill 46% of POCs early, focusing resources on what scales
2. **Full-stack integration** — Not bolt-on tools, but workflow-embedded AI
3. **Measurement discipline** — They define ROI before they build, not after

The question isn't whether to adopt AI. Everyone already has.

The question is: Are you capturing value, or just capturing headlines?
