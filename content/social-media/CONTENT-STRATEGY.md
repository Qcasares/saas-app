## Content Formats

### Thought-Provoking Questions
End every post with a specific question to drive engagement:
- "What's your ROI framework for AI investments?"
- "What on-chain metrics are you watching this week?"
- "How are you handling duplicate detection in your pipeline?"

### Data-Driven Insights
Include real numbers and statistics:
- "88% adoption vs 5-10% ROI — here's the gap"
- "1,908 BTC moved from dormant wallets in February"
- "40-point gap between leading and lagging AI sectors"

## Engagement Tactics

1. **Specific questions** — Not "thoughts?" but "What chunking threshold are you using?"
2. **Name names** — Reference other experts, tools, companies when relevant
3. **Real numbers** — Quantify claims with data
4. **Thread starters** — Use X threads for complex topics
5. **Cross-reference** — Link between platforms where appropriate
